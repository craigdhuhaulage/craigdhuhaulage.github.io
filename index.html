Reducing Your Carbon Footprint With Us

At Craig Dhu Haulage, we pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability. We firmly believe that mere claims of environmental responsibility are insufficient; instead, we take an active approach to minimize our carbon footprint and reduce our impact on the environment.

To achieve this goal, we have invested in state-of-the-art equipment that is highly efficient and incorporates the latest technologies available. We prioritize proper truck specifications, as we understand that inadequately spec'd trucks can lead to a lack of efficiency and increased risks of breakdowns, which in turn can compromise the safety of your freight.

Are carriers dispatching trucks and trailers to pick up your loads with all five axles on the ground when three would suffice! We take a different approach. Once your freight is loaded and on its way to your customer, we ensure that we use the minimum number of axles required - three, four, or five. We go above and beyond industry standards by equipping both our trucks and trailers with lift axles, which significantly reduces our environmental impact and improves fuel efficiency.
Moreover, we have installed several devices to further reduce our carbon footprint while transporting your freight. For example, we use an Electronic APU integrated with a 380w solar panel to reduce idle time and have implemented additional aerodynamics on both the truck and trailer. These upgrades may not be inexpensive, but we believe they are necessary for the future.

At Craig Dhu Haulage, we are committed to providing our clients with the most efficient and environmentally responsible transportation solutions.
