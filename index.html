ABOUT US

Craig Dhu Haulage

We give the service the competition can only talk about. How? When you call, you talk directly with the driver every time to discuss the particulars and needs of your load from pickup to delivery. Know your precious cargo is in the hands of a highly experienced driver every time.

Can We Haul Your Load?

If your freight fits in a standard 53' dry van then yes we can pick up and deliver your freight, LTL, or full load. We can haul from 47 states in the US (not California) to anywhere in Canada, from Canada to elsewhere in Canada, or from Canada to the US. We also have a new food grade max cube drive van, with 101" inside loading possible. Give us a call or send us an email. We look forward to helping you serve your customers better.

Why Should We Trust Craig Dhu Haulage With Our Freight?

Craig Dhu Haulage is a small company 1 truck and 1 trailer. We want the load that means the most to you and to your customer and must get to where it's going on time. I have been in the transportation industry delivering freight for over 25 years, if it can be done we can do it. If we can't we will tell you. I carry a plethora of equipment in the trailers and know how to use them for securement and unloading aids. Our equipment is very well maintained and reliable. I specialize in on time, on-site driver assist deliveries; since July 2015 we have successfully picked up and delivered more than 600 loads on time and damage free.
